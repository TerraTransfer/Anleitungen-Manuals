4G Rod Antenna 280 mm.

Outdoor · L-bracket · FME · 3 dBi

Description

The 4G rod antenna 280 mm is the solution for sites with weak network coverage, deep manholes or metal enclosures — wherever the antenna has to be brought outside the measurement spot into open radio space.

The 280 mm rod on a TPEE base is mounted via a galvanised L-bracket to a mast, enclosure wall or fence. 3 m of LMR195 coax with FME connector is supplied as standard; 1.5 m and 5 m cable variants are available ex stock.

With 3 dBi gain, VSWR ≤ 3.0 and up to 100 W admitted power, the antenna is sized for robust outdoor use. Frequency range 824 – 960 MHz and 1710 – 2700 MHz.

Typical applications

  • Measurement sites in deep manholes or basements
  • Locations at the cell edge or in low-coverage regions
  • Loggers inside metallic enclosures or cabinets
  • Mast mounting (gauge mast, antenna mast, lamp post)
  • Wall or railing mount via L-bracket
  • Replacement for older, poorly receiving antenna combinations

Use with Aquatos Web LTX

Connection to the Aquatos Web LTX directly via the FME connector of the LMR195 cable — no adapter required. The rod antenna is mounted outside the measurement manhole and routed to the logger by coax cable.

Compatibility

Recommended for sites with weak cellular reception or loggers in metal enclosures. Two cable options: 1.5 m (short mount) and 5 m (deep manholes, mast distance).

Accessories & mounting

Optional: mast clamps, wall brackets, longer LMR cables on request, FME extensions, lightning arresters for exposed mast sites.
